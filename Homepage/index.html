About Us

Introduction

In order to address the needs of quality education in higher secondary level in lahan, Little Star Secondary English School was established in 2055 Bs with Science stream. In response to the demands of the students and parents, Little Star extended its horizon to include Management stream in 2062 BS. In more than a decade long journey, we have been able to maintain a consistent record of progressive success in both Science and Management streams. Our achievements is marked by the Regional Awards that we earned in 2062, 2064 and 2065 BS for our outstanding results among the private and public institutions in the Eastern Region under the Higher Secondary Education Board of Nepal. It has been maintaining its good position in Eastern Development Region since 2061 BS.

Welcome to our school

Little star is a coeducational day and boarding school for grades I to XII. We welcome and enroll students of all faiths, races, and backgrounds, and offer various need-based scholarships each year. We have small class size which enables staff to build strong relationship with the children, know their families well and work teachers and parents together as a team for the benefit of the children in our care. Each child is fully supported at each stage of learning.
